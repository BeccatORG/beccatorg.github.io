About Us

Beccat is an organization dedicated to rescuing and caring for stray cats. Our mission is to provide them with a safe shelter, medical care, and find them a permanent home where they can live happily.

We believe every cat deserves a dignified and loving life. Through our efforts and with the help of volunteers and donations, we tirelessly work to change the lives of these felines.

We have also developed a game called "Beccat" with the aim of raising funds to cover the shelter’s expenses and help more cats. Every game played directly supports our cause!

We offer a sponsorship program, where volunteers can finance and directly help a cat while it stays at our shelter. This program allows sponsors to visit the cats, assist in their care, and contribute financially until they find a permanent home.
